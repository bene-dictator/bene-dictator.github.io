Someday you'll be reminded

Posted: 16/10/2025

Until only recently I thought my emotionality was a burden, something that got the better of me, and that people who could live without needing to worry about others knew a much simpler and ordered world in which they were always in control.

But now I know I prefer the world where my feelings, strange and unscrutable as they are, connect me to everything and everyone around me- as opposed to the world where my thoughts are determined by a set of instructions, and where my possibilities are blinded and muted by a veil of warmth that hides a unknowable being underneath.

Photo taken at 12:42am, 10th of August 2025 at the Purau Saddle, after a very stressful drive home involving three people in a two-person car. (Some day I will recount this story.)
